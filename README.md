# portfolio
A portfolio is a curated collection of materials—such as work samples, documents, or financial assets—that showcases an individual’s skills, achievements, or investment holdings.
It is used to demonstrate qualifications to potential employers or to manage investment risks through diversification, appearing as digital websites, physical folders, or financial, project, or artist portfolios.

Usage Examples & Types
Professional/Creative Portfolio: A website or folder displaying creative work (e.g., photography, design), case studies, and a resume to attract clients or hiring managers.
Financial Portfolio: A collection of stocks, bonds, cash, and other assets held by an investor or firm to manage risk and return, including types like aggressive or defensive portfolios.
Academic/Career Portfolio: A collection of projects, certificates, and work samples demonstrating professional development.
Project Portfolio: A collection of projects within an organization managed to achieve strategic goals.
